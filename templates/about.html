Description About Us

FaceSense AI is an intelligent emotion-analysis platform designed to help users understand their mood through multiple input modes. The system supports camera-based expression analysis, text-based emotion analysis, and direct mood selection. This multi-input approach makes the platform reliable and convenient for different situations.

For camera mode, FaceSense AI captures face frames, validates visibility, and uses DeepFace to classify dominant emotions such as happy, sad, angry, fear, surprise, and neutral. For text mode, the platform analyzes user language patterns and emotional keywords to detect mood. For manual mode, users can directly select how they are feeling for quick input.

Every mood result is saved securely to user history. The dashboard then transforms this history into meaningful visuals like the Past Mood Journey graph, frequency tracking, and streak indicators. Along with emotion detection, FaceSense AI also provides suggestions to support reflection, wellbeing, and better daily emotional awareness.
